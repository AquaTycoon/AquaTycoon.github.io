Play the best free browser tycoon game — no download, no login required

HydroSafe Tycoon is a free idle tycoon game and incremental clicker where you build and manage a water empire. Start with a simple water well and grow to control desalination plants, UV sterilizers, nano purifiers, and aqua resorts across 7 land parcels including River Delta, Industrial Zone, Coastal Strip, Mountain Valley, Desert Flats, and Arctic Reserve.

This tycoon simulator includes a full tech tree, reputation system, contracts and deals, random events like droughts and monsoons, terrain restrictions, rebirth prestige mechanic, and WebGL water animation. Play at 1x, 2x, or 4x speed. Auto-saves progress.
